# Logo candidate discovery returns logos from other companies (carousel/partner images on homepage)

## Problem
Logo candidate discovery returns logos from other companies (carousel/partner images on homepage)

## Solution
Large companies often feature partner/client logos in homepage carousels. Always visually verify every logo candidate - do not trust role/score alone. The og:image meta tag is often the most reliable source for the company's own logo.
